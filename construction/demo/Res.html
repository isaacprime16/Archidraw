MEMBLEY RESIDENTIAL

The renovation and expansion of a luxurious mansionette will seamlessly blend classic elegance with modern sophistication. The project aims to preserve the mansionette's inherent charm while introducing contemporary design elements, creating a haven of opulence for its discerning homeowners. Skilled architects will collaborate to enhance the architectural beauty, while interior designers will curate exquisite spaces with high-end finishes and bespoke decor. Structural enhancements will ensure the mansionette's longevity, and technological integration will introduce smart home automation systems.

The outdoor environment will be transformed with beautiful gardens and outdoor entertainment areas. The project promises a timeless and refined living experience, combining craftsmanship, technology, and design in perfect harmony.

PROJECT DETAIL

  • Client:Florence Njeri Muraki
  • Architect: David Munene Thigiti
  • Builder: Archidraw Associates
  • Budget: 12,000,000
